# Metrics – HEART & GSM

## HEART Dimensions
- Happiness (satisfaction, NPS, SUS)
- Engagement (frequency, intensity, depth)
- Adoption (new users/features used)
- Retention (returning usage, churn)
- Task Success (efficiency, effectiveness, error rate)
